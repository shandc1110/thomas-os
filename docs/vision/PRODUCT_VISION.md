# Chosen by Chloe OS — Product Vision

Chosen by Chloe OS is the operational backbone for Chosen by Chloe — a single platform that manages products, inventory, orders, fulfilment, and future sales channels.

## Principles

1. **Single source of truth** — Inventory lives in the portal, not spreadsheets.
2. **Immutable audit trail** — Every stock change is logged; balances are derived from movements.
3. **Channel-agnostic** — Shopify, WeChat, Amazon, and Xiaohongshu connect to the same product master.
4. **Warehouse-first** — Stock is tracked per location, supporting multiple warehouses today and more tomorrow.
